# Product Brief: SpeakMate

## 1. Vision & Mission
**SpeakMate** is an AI-first EdTech platform designed to empower Vietnamese high school students and young learners to overcome the fear of public speaking and build critical thinking skills. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality, personalized communication coaching, turning the anxiety of speaking into the joy of expression.

## 2. Problem Statement
Many Vietnamese students struggle with:
*   **Fear of Judgment:** Afraid to speak up in class or communicate in English due to fear of making mistakes.
*   **Lack of Practice Environment:** Limited opportunities to practice speaking in safe, non-judgmental scenarios.
*   **Passive Learning:** Traditional education focuses on grammar and reading, neglecting active communication and critical thinking.
*   **"Virtual Trap":** Students may be good at online exercises but fail to apply skills in real-world interactions.

## 3. Solution Overview
SpeakMate provides a safe, AI-driven practice environment with three core modes:
1.  **Safe Mode (Conversation):** A judgment-free zone to practice daily social interactions with a friendly AI Mentor.
2.  **Stage Mode (Presentation):** A structured environment to practice formal speeches with feedback on timing, clarity, and engagement.
3.  **Debate Mode (Critical Thinking):** A challenging arena to sharpen logic and argumentation skills against an AI Opponent.

**Key Differentiator:** The **"Boomerang Loop"** - a strategy that pushes users to apply what they learn in the real world through "Secret Quests" and return to the app for debriefing.

## 4. Target Audience
*   **Primary:** Vietnamese High School Students (15-18 years old) & University Freshmen.
*   **Psychographics:** Introverted, anxious about speaking, ambitious but lacking confidence, or competitive students wanting to sharpen debate skills.

## 5. Key Success Metrics (MVP)
*   **User Success:** Completing a 3-turn conversation without excessive "undo" actions.
*   **Engagement:** Day 1 Retention > 30%.
*   **Technical:** Push-to-Talk latency < 3s; Zero-PII compliance.

## 6. Roadmap Strategy
*   **MVP-0 (The Safe Place):** Guest mode, Safe Mode only, Push-to-Talk. Focus on core value.
*   **MVP-1 (The Growth Engine):** Social Login, Stage/Debate modes, Actionable Feedback, Secret Quests.
*   **Phase 2:** Real-time streaming, Teacher Dashboard, Gamification.
*   **Phase 3:** Video analysis, Mobile Apps.
